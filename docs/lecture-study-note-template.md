# Lecture Study Note Template

## Goal

This template is for turning lecture subtitles, transcripts, or course notes into Obsidian-ready Markdown study notes.
The output should not be a short summary.
It should be a study note that can replace watching the original lecture.

## Default Output

Unless the user asks for another format, the default output is an Obsidian Markdown study note.

## Output Principles

- Preserve key concepts.
- Preserve explanations.
- Preserve examples.
- Preserve numbers and concrete details.
- Preserve comparisons.
- Preserve missions, exercises, and action steps.
- Remove meaningless repeated phrases, greetings, filler, and promotional repetition.
- Do not over-compress the lecture.
- Do not invent facts not present in the source.
- Write in clear beginner-friendly language.
- Keep the structure stable across runs.

## Internal Review Criteria

- Can someone understand the lecture without watching the original video?
- Are key concepts included?
- Are explanations included?
- Are examples included?
- Are numbers and concrete details preserved?
- Are comparisons included?
- Are missions or exercises included?
- Are action steps included?
- Is the output useful for review later?
- Is the structure stable?
- Are unsupported claims avoided?
